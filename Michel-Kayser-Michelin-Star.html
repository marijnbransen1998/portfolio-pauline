Michel Kayser: Gastronomic Influencer Event

Project Overview

To celebrate the anniversary of Michel Kayser's two-Michelin-starred restaurant near Marseille, I organized an exclusive influencer event. The aim was to engage culinary influencers and bring them into the world of Chef Kayser, enhancing the restaurant's prestige and digital presence.

Main Tasks

  • Influencer Targeting: Identified and engaged influencers specializing in gastronomy and fine dining.
  • Event Planning: Coordinated all aspects of the influencer trip, including travel arrangements and itinerary.
  • Post-Event Reporting: Analyzed and reported on the event's impact and social media reach.

Results

The event resulted in over 80 Instagram stories and two dedicated reels, significantly boosting the restaurant's visibility and online engagement within the culinary community.
