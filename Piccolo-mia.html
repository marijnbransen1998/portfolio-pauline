Piccola Mia: Restaurant Launch Event

Project Overview

Piccola Mia, a new Italian restaurant in Paris, opened its doors in January 2024. Tasked with managing their PR and influencer strategy, I organized a high-profile launch event to introduce the restaurant to the city's vibrant food scene.

Main Tasks

  • PR and Influencer Strategy: Developed a comprehensive strategy to maximize media coverage and social media buzz.
  • Guest Management: Successfully invited and coordinated with over 100 journalists and influencers for the grand opening.
  • Event Planning: Managed the entire launch event, including logistics, catering, entertainment, and hospitality.

Results

The launch was a resounding success, leading to increased bookings and substantial media coverage. The event's impact was reflected in the restaurant's heightened profile and increased popularity, setting a strong foundation for its future success.

  • Influencers and journalists: 80
  • Content: 188 stories, 4 IG posts
  • Articles: 10 including web and print media
  • Estimated Impressions: 1.9M
  • Earned Media Value: 150K€
