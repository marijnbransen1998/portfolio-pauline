Coca Cola: Food Festival Launch

Project Overview

In summer 2024, Coca Cola launched its inaugural food court in Paris, featuring 10 chefs from various countries. The objective was to create significant buzz and establish the venue as a culinary hotspot through strategic influencer marketing.

Main Tasks

  • Influencer Sourcing: Identified and engaged international lifestyle and food influencers to participate in the campaign.
  • Negotiation and Coordination: Facilitated discussions between influencers and Coca Cola to create engaging Instagram and TikTok content.
  • Content Strategy: Developed and executed a plan for influencers to produce and share content highlighting the food festival.

Results

The campaign generated widespread interest and online buzz, positioning the Coca Cola food court as a premier destination for food enthusiasts in Paris. The strategic use of influencer marketing effectively increased the brand's reach and engagement across social media platforms.
